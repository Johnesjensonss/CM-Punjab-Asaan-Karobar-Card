Eligibility Criteria for CM Punjab Asaan Karobar Card

Before applying, ensure you meet the following criteria:

  • Age: 21 to 57 years old.
  • Resident of Punjab, Pakistan.
  • Valid CNIC and a registered mobile number.
  • Existing business owner or planning to start a business.
  • No overdue loans or credit defaults.

Step-by-Step Application Process

Follow these steps to apply successfully:

  1. Gather required documents, including CNIC, proof of business, and references.
  2. Register on the official portal: CM Punjab Asaan Karobar Card Portal.
  3. Complete the online application form with accurate details.
  4. Pay the PKR 500 processing fee through online banking or at bank counters.
  5. Submit your application and track its progress using your reference number.

Loan Benefits and Features

Once approved, applicants receive:

  • Interest-free loan up to PKR 1 million.
  • Revolving credit with flexible repayment options.
  • Business growth support through government-backed funding.
